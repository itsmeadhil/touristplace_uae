Aqua Bounce Ajman
Aqua Bounce Ajman is the perfect entertainment destination to spend your time in Ajman. It is a floating water park equipped for tourists with all public facilities such as shops and restaurants, as well as many fun water games such as slides, water towers, bridges suspended in the water, and trampoline jumping, which is a suitable place With all ages for young and old, do not hesitate to take your friends and family with you for a fun day of fun .
Timing:   7:00 AM to 6:00 PM
Entry Fee:  AED 50 and AED 25 for Children
Review:  3.9 out of 5  
UAE Pygmy Zoo
The UAE Pygmy Zoo is the first of its kind attraction in Ajman. Although some parts of the zoo are still under construction, the venue is open for visitors. It’s a particularly popular spot for families with young kids. Here, the kids can enjoy the outdoors and learn about various species of animals, birds and reptiles. There’s a whole bunch of things to do at this petting zoo, including animal encounters, camel and pony rides, and playing with cute little pups.
Timing:   3:00 PM to 12:00 PM
Entry Fee:   Adult: AED 15, Child: AED 10
Review:  4.3 out of 5  
Al Zorah Natural Reserve
Al Zorah natural reserve Ajman is located in the coastal suburb of Ajman. It’s a beautiful and vibrant ecosystem spread across one million square meters of lively Al Zorah mangrove, sandy lagoons, beaches, and tidal creeks. This place is home to around 103 species of birds like flamingos, herons and is a shelter to many migratory birds around the world like Wasit wetland in Sharjah. You can read about Wasit wetland at the last.
Timing:   9 AM To 6 PM
Entry Fee:   FREE Entrance and Mangrove Tour: AED 150 Wakeboarding: AED 100
Review:  4.2 out of 5

Al Hamriya Beach
Al Hamriyah Beach is a pristine beach with clear waters and smooth white sand. It is a nice place to visit especially when you prefer a quiet place as it is less crowded. Swimming is allowed on the beach and it also has lifeguards on duty. The beach has a 1,300-metre jogging track, a service building and an interlock walking track that is 3 metres wide. There is a kids play area and a separate place for barbeque. Public toilets and showers are available at the beach for the visitor's convenience. The place is fabulous recommended for families.
Timing:   6 AM - 7 AM
Entry Fee:   FREE
Review:  4.5 out of 5
